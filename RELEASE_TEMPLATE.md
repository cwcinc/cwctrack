## Download

### Windows
**[CWCTrack Setup {{VERSION}}.exe](https://github.com/{{REPO}}/releases/download/{{TAG}}/CWCTrack.Setup.{{VERSION}}.exe)** - Installer (recommended)

Open the `.exe`. On the windows warning page, **click `More info` and `Run anyway`**. Follow prompts to finish the install.

### macOS
**[CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}-arm64.dmg](https://github.com/{{REPO}}/releases/download/{{TAG}}/CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}-arm64.dmg)** - Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4)

**[CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}.dmg](https://github.com/{{REPO}}/releases/download/{{TAG}}/CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}.dmg)** - Intel Macs

Open the `.dmg`, drag CWCTrack to Applications, then **right-click the app and choose Open** the first time. Click "Open" in the dialog.
### Linux
**[CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}.AppImage](https://github.com/{{REPO}}/releases/download/{{TAG}}/CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}.AppImage)** - Portable (most distros)

```bash
chmod +x CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}.AppImage
./CWCTrack-{{VERSION}}.AppImage
```
